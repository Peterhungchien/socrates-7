- Limited prior exposure to deep learning — has seen neural networks mentioned in coursework

---

## Learning Goals

1. Build rigorous, first-principles understanding of deep learning — not just how to use
   frameworks, but why the math works.
2. Understand gradient-based optimization deeply: backpropagation derivation, vanishing
   gradients, and the geometry of loss surfaces.
3. Connect DL architectures to statistical concepts already understood (e.g., bias-variance,
   regularization, probabilistic models).
4. Develop the theoretical grounding needed to read and evaluate DL research papers.
